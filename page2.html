Overall, this project is meant to raise awareness of framing and its potential upsides and downsides. However, there's a lot more examples and versions of framing that can't be accurately depicted in the form of a questionnaire. All of these questions were inspired by Daniel Kahneman's book Thinking Fast and Slow. There's also online resources that can be helpful in learning more:

BoyceWire: Framing Effect Definition
Medium: Framing Bias, Examples, and Decision-Making in Business
